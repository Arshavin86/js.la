Presenting the new js.la, A monthly JS meet up in Los Angeles.

We meet the last Thursday of every month and alternate months throughout the Los Angeles area. Generally, odd numbered months (such as March, May, and July) meet in the Hollywood neighborhood while even numbered months (such as April, June, and August) meet in Santa Monica.

The general format is two planned talks followed by a couple of pretty short lightning talks and socializing, and of course beer. We are always looking for speakers. If you are interested, or want to help out in other ways, please let us know via twitter.


April 26th, 2012 - Santa Monica

7pm, with talks starting at 7:20p - 7:30p

Introducing Mojito

Presented by Gamaiel Zavala, Yahoo

Mojito is a sweet (and minty!) MVC application framework built on YUI 3 that enables agile development of Web applications. Gamaiel Zavala will be presenting an introduction to Mojito including set up, routing, controllers, models, addons, binders, and configuration. Gamaiel has been building Media sites at Yahoo! for 6 years and is currently working on Media's migration to Mojito.

Seeing Sound with HTML5

Presented by David Guttman

Embrace JavaScript-induced synesthesia. Thanks to recent advances in web audio API technology, you can now see sound---in your browser. Thus, the time for in-browser music visualization is now! It's easier than ever before with EasyEars.js and David will show you how.
